Frequently Asked Questions

More questions? Contact Mr. Zhang: +86-177-6988-6009

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Standard MOQ is 100 meters per design. Trial orders for new customers can start at 50 meters. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What is the maximum printing width?

J.LINK TEXTILE's maximum printing width is 320CM. This ultra-wide capability is rare in the industry and ideal for seamless curtains, full-wall wallcoverings, and wide-format home textiles.

What's the difference between reactive and disperse printing?

Reactive printing: for cotton, linen, silk (natural fibers). Soft hand feel, excellent color fastness, ideal for bedding and cotton curtains.
Disperse printing: for polyester and synthetic fabrics. Vibrant colors, UV resistant, ideal for blackout curtains, wallcoverings, and technical textiles.
Tell us your fabric composition and we'll recommend the right process.

What is the lead time?

Sampling takes 1-2 working days. Bulk orders are completed in 5-7 working days after artwork approval and order confirmation. Rush production is available upon request.

Do you offer free sampling?

Yes. First-time customers receive free sampling (1-2 designs). Shipping costs apply. Sample fees are deductible from bulk order payments. Please provide your fabric for accurate sampling results.

How do you ensure color accuracy?

We use an 8-color ink system with professional ICC color management. Color difference from sample: ΔE < 1.5. Color fastness ≥ Grade 4. Full inspection reports available upon request.
